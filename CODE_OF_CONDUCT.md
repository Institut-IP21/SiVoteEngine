# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.

We are committed to a harassment-free, welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background or
identity. We expect participants to be respectful, assume good faith, and focus on what is best for the
community and for the integrity of a system people rely on to run fair elections.

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, personal attacks, and publishing others' private
information. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that violate this code,
and may ban offenders.

**Reporting:** report unacceptable behaviour to **info@ip21.si**. Reports are handled confidentially.

The full text and enforcement guidelines are at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.
